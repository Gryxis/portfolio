Evolving L Systems

This is a Turtle which will draw some evolutions of L-Systems.

This means that you will tell a turtle where it shall go to step-by-step only defining a rule, and this rule will be re-interpreted for every expansion.

Therefore here are some background informations:

The turtle has a direction it looks at and a position. You can the turtle to turn left or right by a fixed degree. And you can tell the turtle to take one step into the direction. At the end I will show you which way the turtle took.

A L-System is a Grammar which has one function. And the function will lead to a combination of 'F', '-', '+', '[' or ']' End for each expansion any 'F' will be replaced by the value of the function. e.g. the Function F -> F - F will lead to F-F by one expansion, F-F - F-F by two expansion and so forth.
